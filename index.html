IVA POS - Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-02

1. Introduction

IVA POS ("the app") is developed by IVA ("we"). If you have questions, contact iva.backoffice@gmail.com.

2. Data we handle

  • Google account email when you sign in for Google Sheets/Drive sync.
  • Login information (email and password) when you create an account or sign in.
  • Business data you enter: products, categories, invoices, invoice items, revenue, invoice notes.
  • Store info you enter: store name, address, phone, thank-you text.
  • Bank transfer info you enter: bank name, BIN, account number, account name.
  • Printing settings: printer type, encoding, paper size, print configuration.
  • Invoice images when you choose to generate/share them (optional).

3. Data we do NOT collect

We do not collect location, contacts, SMS, microphone, advertising ID, or other sensitive data beyond the items above.

4. How we use data

  • Provide POS features, printing, QR payments, and reports.
  • Optional sync to Google Sheets/Drive when you enable Google sync.
  • App protection with PIN lock (if enabled).
  • Account authentication and subscription management (when you sign in).

5. Data sharing

  • We do not operate our own servers to store your data.
  • When you enable Google sync, data is stored in your own Google Sheets/Drive.
  • When you share invoice images to Drive, the file may be set to "anyone with the link".
  • Authentication is handled by Firebase Authentication (Google). We do not store your password.

6. Storage and security

  • Data is stored locally on your device.
  • Network connections use HTTPS when applicable.
  • You are responsible for protecting your device and Google account.
  • Account data is protected according to Firebase and Google policies.

7. Retention and deletion

  • Local data remains until you delete it in the app or uninstall.
  • Synced data can be deleted from your Google account (Sheets/Drive).
  • For help, contact support.

8. Children

The app is intended for business use and is not directed to children.

9. Changes

We may update this policy. The effective date will be updated if changes occur.

10. Contact

IVA - iva.backoffice@gmail.com

1. Gioi thieu

IVA POS ("ung dung") do IVA ("chung toi") phat trien. Neu ban co cau hoi, vui long lien he: iva.backoffice@gmail.com.

2. Du lieu chung toi xu ly

  • Thong tin dang nhap (email, mat khau) khi ban tao tai khoan hoac dang nhap.
  • Du lieu kinh doanh: san pham, danh muc, hoa don, chi tiet hoa don, doanh thu, ghi chu.

  • Thong tin chuyen khoan: ten ngan hang, BIN, so tai khoan, ten chu tai khoan.
  • Cau hinh in an: loai may in, ma hoa, kho giay, cau hinh in.

3. Du lieu chung toi khong thu thap

Chung toi khong thu thap vi tri, danh ba, tin nhan SMS, micro, danh tinh quang cao, hoac du lieu nhay cam khac ngoai nhung muc da neu.

4. Muc dich su dung du lieu

  • Dong bo tuy chon len Google Sheets/Drive khi ban bat tinh nang dong bo.
  • Bao mat ung dung bang khoa PIN (neu bat).
  • Xac thuc tai khoan va quan ly goi dich vu (neu dang nhap).

5. Chia se du lieu

  • Chung toi khong co may chu rieng de luu tru du lieu nguoi dung.

6. Luu tru va bao mat

  • Du lieu duoc luu cuc bo tren thiet bi.
  • Ket noi mang su dung HTTPS (neu co).
  • Ban can bao ve thiet bi va tai khoan Google cua minh.

7. Luu giu va xoa du lieu

  • Du lieu cuc bo ton tai cho den khi ban xoa trong ung dung hoac go cai dat.
